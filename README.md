👨‍💼 HR Analytics Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to help Human Resource teams analyze workforce demographics, salary distribution, employee growth, leave balances, and workforce trends. The dashboard provides actionable insights that support data-driven HR decisions regarding recruitment, compensation, workforce planning, and employee management.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze employee demographics and workforce composition.
Monitor salary distribution across different job roles.
Identify top earners within each department.
Compare employee qualifications with salary levels.
Track employee hiring trends over time.
Analyze leave balances across employees.
Enable dynamic employee filtering for quick search and analysis.


📊 Dashboard Features
1. Employee Distribution by Job Role
Displays the total number of employees in each job position.
Helps HR understand workforce allocation across departments.
2. Gender Breakdown
Visualizes the distribution of male and female employees.
Supports diversity and inclusion analysis.
3. Age Distribution
Shows employee age groups and workforce demographics.
Helps HR identify workforce maturity and succession planning needs.
4. Salary Analysis by Job Role
Compares average salaries across different positions.
Identifies high-paying and low-paying job roles.
5. Top Earners by Job
Displays the highest-paid employees within each designation.
Useful for compensation benchmarking.
6. Qualification vs Salary
Compares employee education qualifications with salary levels.
Helps evaluate compensation based on educational background.
7. Employee Growth Trend
Tracks employee hiring trends over time.
Assists HR in workforce planning and recruitment analysis.
8. Employee Search by Initial Letter
Dynamic filter to search employees based on the first letter of their names.
Improves dashboard usability.
9. Leave Balance Analysis
Analyzes remaining leave balances across employees.
Helps monitor employee leave utilization.
